Carbon Dioxide

Global CO2 concentrations from May through Oct. 2016. In May, CO2 levels reached a global record high exceeding 404 parts per million. 2016 marks the first year where global CO2 concentrations will not drop below 400 PPM, a mark that will not be reached again without a dramatic reduction in global CO2 emissions.
