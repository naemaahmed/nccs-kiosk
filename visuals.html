Temperature Anomalies

30-day running air temperature anomalies from the 1980-2010 climatology of MERRA-2 for the period from February through October 2016. The high resolution of this analysis allows us to see the details of record warmth that spread across the globe in 2016. Credits: William Putman, NASA/GMAO.

Carbon Dioxide

Global CO2 concentrations from May through Oct. 2016. In May, CO2 levels reached a global record high exceeding 404 parts per million. 2016 marks the first year where global CO2 concentrations will not drop below 400 PPM, a mark that will not be reached again without a dramatic reduction in global CO2 emissions. Credits: William Putman, NASA/GMAO.

Visualization Wall

The NCCS Data Exploration Theater features a 20-foot by 6-foot 10-inch multi-screen visualization wall, also known as a hyperwall. The NCCS visualization wall/hyperwall combines 15 55-inch high-definition LCD screens with very narrow bezels, resulting in an almost seamless surface of more than 31 million pixels.
